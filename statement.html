Statement
In my life, I have carefully observed the balance of order and chaos in our world and time. My work explores the current of divine energy which animates our material world. Although not endorsing a particular spiritual tradition, my paintings draw on allegories and symbolic representations to provide the viewer with a deeper insight of where the sacred touches the profane. I consider my work a conversation on what makes our existence meaningful and invite anyone interested to participate.
