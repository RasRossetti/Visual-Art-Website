About The Artist

An artist of the American south, Ras Rossetti applies paint to canvas in order to make sense of the world around him. His journey has taken him all over the globe, utilizing every tool at his disposal to tease meaning from a world transforming more rapidly by the day. In his explorations, he would augment his family's rich artistic lineage with studies in esoteric sciences, alternative medical modalities and eastern philosophy. Please explore his website and let us know if you find something you like.
